WhisperIME turns speech into text entirely on your phone, using OpenAI's Whisper
models running locally. Nothing you say ever leaves the device.

It works three ways: as a standalone app, as a system-wide keyboard (IME) which can
e.g. be activated via the microphone button in HeliBoard, and as your device's voice
input service.

Features:
<ul>
<li>Fully offline — the internet permission is only used to download the model you choose</li>
<li>Two engines: TensorFlow Lite and whisper.cpp (GGUF), picked automatically by the model</li>
<li>Model catalog with per-model download, resume, cancel, delete and a Wi-Fi-only option</li>
<li>Unlimited dictation — no 30-second cap, speech is split at pauses and transcribed as you go</li>
<li>Recognition history with search; password fields are never recorded</li>
<li>Material You design: four palettes plus dynamic color, light/dark/system</li>
<li>Quick Settings tile, home-screen widget, and a Dictate action in the text-selection menu</li>
<li>As a standalone app it can also translate any supported language to English</li>
</ul>

To get the most out of WhisperIME:
<ul>
<li>Press and hold the button while speaking</li>
<li>Pause briefly before starting to speak</li>
<li>Speak clearly, loudly, and at a moderate pace</li>
</ul>
